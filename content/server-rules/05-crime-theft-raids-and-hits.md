# Crime, Theft, Raids, and Hits

## In One Breath

Crime is the engine. Random murder is sand in the engine. Bring a motive, use the right tools, and accept the chase.

## Rules

### CRIME-001 - Criminal tools require criminal context

Lockpicks, keypad crackers, pickpocket tools, ATM hacking tools, and robbery actions must be used for valid criminal RP.

Do not swing them at random doors, players, cars, or public fixtures just to force reactions.

### CRIME-002 - Lockpicking or cracking makes you a threat

If you are actively breaching an owned base, vehicle, or protected door, the owner and valid defenders may treat you as hostile.

Do not pretend an active lockpick is peaceful sightseeing.

### CRIME-003 - Raids must have a clear target

A raid is an attempt to enter, steal from, damage, or contest a specific base or protected area.

Raids should start from a real entrance or breach path. Do not declare vague citywide raids, hidden helper raids, or raids with no target.

### CRIME-004 - No inside raiding

Do not join a base, receive access, get co-owner, join an organization, or ask to be let in just to immediately betray that access.

If you are already legitimately inside for unrelated RP, leave and start a proper raid from outside.

### CRIME-005 - Base defenders must be connected to the base

You may defend a base if you own it, co-own it, share access through an organization, are actively basing there, or are clearly part of the raid/defense scene.

"I know him" is not enough by itself.

### CRIME-006 - Countering must be connected to the scene

You may counter a crime when you are law enforcement, partied or organized with the victim through a real server system, defending your property, or directly included in the scene.

Do not use "counter" as a global license to sprint into every gunfight.

### CRIME-007 - Do not edit a base during an active raid

Once a raid or breach is active, do not add props, move props, change fading doors, change keypads, relock opened world doors, hide valuables through build tools, or restructure the base.

Finish the scene with the base you built.

### CRIME-008 - Pickpocketing follows the pickpocket system

Only eligible thief roles with the correct tool may pickpocket, and the target, range, cooldown, and wallet checks must pass.

If the system denies the attempt, do not recreate it with harassment, body blocking, or random damage.

### CRIME-009 - ATM hacking is an obvious crime

ATM hacking must use the ATM hacker role/tool flow and a valid ATM target. A successful hack can apply wanted consequences and police response.

Do not body block the ATM, exploit stuck states, or farm one location through bugs.

### CRIME-010 - Bank robbery follows the vault rules

Thieves or Pro Thieves start bank robberies. Same-organization gangsters and mob bosses may assist when the robbery rules allow it. Law enforcement cannot join the robbery crew.

Robbers must stay in the robbery area and accept removal for death, arrest, disconnect, or leaving too long.

### CRIME-011 - Stolen gold is hot property

Bank gold is part of the robbery scene. Eligible criminals may steal, pocket, drop, persist, and sell it when the system allows. Law enforcement may confiscate world gold.

Do not hide gold through exploits, unreachable props, or out-of-bounds storage.

### CRIME-012 - Hitman kills require active system contracts

Hitmen may kill a target only when the hitman system has an active contract assigning that target to that hitman.

Verbal bounties, joke payments, and "someone told me to" are not valid hit contracts.

### CRIME-013 - Hitmen must stay focused

A hit is permission to kill the target, not everyone nearby. You may defend yourself from immediate attackers, but you may not use the contract to start a public rampage.

### CRIME-014 - Do not dodge criminal consequences

If you start a crime, accept the response. Do not disconnect, job switch, run to spawn, killbind, pocket everything through an exploit, or hide behind staff calls to escape.

### CRIME-015 - Raid baiting and fake crimes are not allowed

Do not fake adverts, fake lockpick sounds, fake distress calls, or fake compliance to bait kills or reports.

Criminals can be clever. They cannot be cheap.

## System Notes

- ATM hacking can apply a 30 second wanted timer.
- Default wanted and warrant timers are 300 seconds.
- Hit prices are currently `$100` to `$50,000`, default `$1,000`.
- Bank robbery crew rules are enforced by `BankRobberyRules`.

# Bases, Building, Doors, and Signage

## In One Breath

Build a fortress, not a spreadsheet trap. Raiders should fight your base, not your interpretation of a decimal.

## Rules

### BASE-001 - Claim a base through real ownership

To claim a base, own or rent the relevant doors and make your claim clear.

If a building has required doors, you should control the doors that make the claim meaningful. A sign alone does not magically own half the map.

### BASE-002 - Keep required map flow open

Do not block spawn, spawn exits, sidewalks needed for basic travel, streets needed for normal traffic, NPCs, job entities, shops, police access, bank vault access, or required map doors.

Staff may remove builds that harm map flow.

### BASE-003 - No prop abuse

Do not prop kill, prop push, prop climb, prop surf, prop shield, prop spam, body block with props, hide inside props, or use props to bypass normal access.

If a prop is doing the job of a rule break, it is still a rule break.

### BASE-004 - Bases must be raidable

If a base stores valuables, players, printers, bitcoin miners, drug machines, weed grows, stolen gold, weapons, or other RP assets, it must be possible to raid through normal tools and movement.

Do not build impossible entrances, invisible routes, one-pixel gaps, forced suicide paths, or uncounterable killboxes.

### BASE-005 - Do not force unnatural movement in the main raid path

The main entry path should not require crouch mazes, precision jumping, hidden buttons, impossible turns, water abuse, or tricks that exist only to make raiding mechanically miserable.

Use defense, not geometry homework.

### BASE-006 - Fading doors must be fair

Fading doors, buttons, and keypads must be visible enough to interact with and connected to a real entrance or defense.

Do not make fully invisible fading doors, hide keypads inside props, place keypad faces where they cannot be used, or bind doors in a way that only works through secret owner knowledge.

### BASE-007 - Keypads and buttons must match the defended door

If a keypad or button controls an entry fading door, it should be placed near that entry and usable by raiders.

Do not place a required keypad across the map, behind another locked layer, or in a location that cannot be reached without already winning the raid.

### BASE-008 - Building signs are for active building only

A building sign protects active construction, not stored profit.

No printers, bitcoin miners, drug machines, weed grows, stolen gold, valuable shipments, or hidden players should be protected under a building sign.

### BASE-009 - KOS signs must be readable and specific

If KOS signs are allowed by current server policy, they must be visible, readable, and describe a real area or condition.

Tiny text, blended colors, hidden signs, and "KOS if I dislike you" do not count.

### BASE-010 - No base edits during raids

Do not move, spawn, remove, recolor, relock, freeze, rebind, or reconfigure base defenses during an active raid.

Raiders and defenders both play the base as it existed when the scene started.

### BASE-011 - Do not weaponize toolgun or physgun access

The toolgun and physgun are for building and cleanup. Do not use them to trap players, block bullets mid-fight, move vehicles with occupants, steal through walls, crash clients, or alter active combat scenes.

### BASE-012 - Signs and screens must follow conduct rules

World text, notice boards, lightboxes, TV screens, and other display tools cannot contain hate speech, staff impersonation, exploit instructions, harassment, or unreadable clutter.

### BASE-013 - Cleanup abandoned builds

If you abandon a base, change jobs, disconnect, or move across town, clean up old props and defenses when possible.

Staff may remove abandoned or harmful builds.

## Examples

- Allowed: a shop with a public counter, a locked back room, and a visible keypad.
- Not allowed: a public shop where the only raid path is a hidden pixel button inside a black wall.
- Allowed: a building sign while you are actively placing walls and have no valuables inside.
- Not allowed: a building sign covering five upgraded printers in a vault.

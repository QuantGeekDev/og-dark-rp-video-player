# Community Conduct

## In One Breath

Be shady in character. Be decent out of character. The server needs villains, not actual creeps.

## Rules

### CONDUCT-001 - No hate speech or discriminatory harassment

Racism, sexism, antisemitism, homophobia, transphobia, slurs, extremist symbols, and targeted identity harassment are not allowed in chat, voice, names, signs, media, props, or builds.

Roleplay is not a costume for real harassment.

### CONDUCT-002 - No threats or malicious behavior

Do not threaten real people, doxx players, encourage self-harm, share malware, attempt account theft, or disrupt the server through external tools.

This includes "jokes" that require staff to guess whether you are joking.

### CONDUCT-003 - No ban evasion or punishment dodging

Do not use alternate accounts, family sharing, VPN tricks, name changes, or Discord hopping to bypass punishments or restrictions.

If you think a punishment was wrong, appeal it through the proper channel.

### CONDUCT-004 - No staff or official impersonation

Do not claim to be staff, pretend to act for staff, fake staff commands, fake official warnings, or use names/tags that make players think you have authority you do not have.

Organizations may roleplay authority only when it is clearly in character.

### CONDUCT-005 - Keep real-money and external trades out of staff enforcement

Trades outside official game systems are at your own risk unless the server later adds a formal trade system.

Staff may still act on scams that involve harassment, real-money fraud, account theft, or malicious behavior.

### CONDUCT-006 - Do not use offensive builds or media

Props, signs, TV/cinema content, drawings, organization tags, and base decorations must follow the same conduct rules as chat.

If staff cannot reasonably leave it in public, do not build it in public.

### CONDUCT-007 - Do not target players out of character

Repeatedly hunting, blocking, mocking, or harassing a player because of OOC drama is not roleplay.

If you have a dispute, report it or avoid them. Do not turn the whole city into your group chat argument.

### CONDUCT-008 - Respect staff spaces and support flow

When staff are handling a report, answer plainly. Do not spam, talk over everyone, destroy evidence, flee the sit, or drag unrelated drama into it.

Staff time is a shared server resource. Spend it wisely.

## Examples

- Allowed: a gangster insults the mayor's tax policy in character.
- Not allowed: using slurs because "my character is rude."
- Allowed: a shady dealer scams someone through a risky in-character sale, if no server rule or official system is being bypassed.
- Not allowed: promising real-money items, taking payment, and vanishing.

# Identity, Chat, and Adverts

## In One Breath

Say what your character would know. Use global channels with restraint. If your plan needs a fake advert, it is probably a bad plan wearing a tiny hat.

## Rules

### CHAT-001 - Use a valid roleplay name

Your RP name must be a first and last name using English letters, hyphens, or apostrophes. It must be readable, non-offensive, and not impersonate staff or another player.

Current name rules:

- exactly first name and last name
- each name part is 2 to 16 characters
- full name is 35 characters or fewer
- no punctuation at the start or end

### CHAT-002 - Keep IC and OOC separate

Local proximity chat, yell, whisper, `/me`, job/group chat, and law radio are in-character unless the channel clearly says otherwise.

`/ooc` is out-of-character. Use it for questions, jokes, and server talk, not for live tactical information.

### CHAT-003 - Do not metagame

Metagaming is using information your character should not know.

Examples:

- calling police because Discord told you who raided you
- using a job label above a player's head as proof they committed a crime
- sending a PM for backup while being mugged
- using health, name, or UI state as perfect in-character evidence

### CHAT-004 - Use adverts for unclear public RP actions

Use `/advert` when a player-started action needs public clarity and is not already obvious or system-tracked.

Good uses:

- announcing a player-run shop sale
- declaring a manual raid or counter when no system event announces it
- warning players away from a clearly claimed base
- starting a public event or protest

Bad uses:

- fake raid adverts
- multi-advert walls
- hiding important text in clutter
- baiting someone into shooting you

### CHAT-005 - System-tracked crimes do not need ceremonial spam

If a system already tracks, announces, or flags the action, you do not need to add ritual advert noise unless staff or the specific rule says otherwise.

Examples:

- ATM hacking can apply wanted consequences.
- Bank robbery has a vault session and police visibility.
- Hitman contracts require the contract system.
- Wanted and warrants use law command flows.

You still need an RP reason for your actions.

### CHAT-006 - Do not abuse PMs

Private messages are not a secret radio for live scenes. Do not use PMs to coordinate raids, call backup, identify attackers, or dodge local roleplay.

PMs are fine for OOC coordination that does not affect an active scene.

### CHAT-007 - Law radio is for law work

Law radio voice and text are for government coordination. Do not use it for spam, music, harassment, personal arguments, or non-law business.

Handcuffed or arrested players cannot use law radio. Do not try to bypass that through PMs or external voice.

### CHAT-008 - Mayor broadcasts must be civic

Mayor broadcasts are for city announcements, laws, lockdowns, taxes, lottery notices, and major public information.

Do not use mayor broadcast for harassment, spam, fake staff messages, or personal arguments.

### CHAT-009 - Voice and media volume must be reasonable

Mic spam, ear-blasting audio, repeated soundboards, or constant music in public spaces may be muted or stopped by staff.

Dedicated media roles and cinema/TV systems still follow conduct rules.

### CHAT-010 - Warnings must be clear and reasonable

If you warn someone to leave your area or stop following you, make it obvious, give them a reasonable chance to comply, and do not use warnings as a cheap kill license.

If they leave, the warning did its job.

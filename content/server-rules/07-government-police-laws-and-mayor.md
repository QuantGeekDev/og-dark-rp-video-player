# Government, Police, Laws, and Mayor

## In One Breath

Government jobs are trusted with powerful tools. Use them to run the city, not to turn the city into your private clipboard.

## Rules

### GOV-001 - Government roles must enforce valid law

Police, SWAT, mayoral support, and other law roles should protect citizens, respond to crimes, enforce valid laws, and use their tools for law work.

Government equipment is not a toy box for personal grudges.

### GOV-002 - No random arrests or mass arrests

Arrests need valid wanted status, a valid arrest warrant, or a clear law enforcement reason.

Do not arrest people for job titles, OOC dislike, insults, standing near crime, or refusing an invalid order.

### GOV-003 - Wanted status needs a real reason

Wanted status should be based on observed crime, reliable RP evidence, approved law request, automatic system consequence, or valid warrant flow.

Do not want someone for "being a thief," "looking suspicious," "he annoyed me," or "Discord said so."

### GOV-004 - Warrants need valid evidence

Search and arrest warrants require a specific RP reason. Mayor, Police Chief, Head of SWAT, and staff can issue or review warrants through the law system.

Do not use warrants to harass bases, fish for crimes without cause, or raid political enemies.

### GOV-005 - Search warrants are not loot permits

A search warrant lets law enforcement search for illegal evidence and enforce law. It does not let officers steal valuables for personal profit.

Illegal items should be confiscated or destroyed through the intended system when possible.

### GOV-006 - Police corruption is not allowed by default

Unless a future corruption mechanic explicitly allows it, government roles may not help criminals raid, protect illegal labs, sell warrants, leak law radio, sabotage police, or intentionally ignore active crime for personal profit.

### GOV-007 - Law radio is official communication

Use law radio for policing, warrants, active threats, custody, bank robberies, mayor security, and department coordination.

Do not use it for spam, music, insults, personal deals, or criminal coordination.

### GOV-008 - Weapon checks must be lawful and proportional

Use weapon checkers under valid police policy, public safety concerns, or law enforcement scenes.

Do not check every player endlessly or bait fights with checks.

### GOV-009 - Gun licenses affect legality, not all server rules

Having a gun license can make weapon possession legal under city law. It does not make RDM, threats, raids, or police abuse legal.

Revoking a license needs valid law authority and reason.

### GOV-010 - Mayor laws cannot override server rules

Invalid laws include:

- laws that permit RDM, random arrest, or KOS as first resort
- laws that target one player by name
- laws that ban normal movement, speech, job choice, or existing server mechanics
- laws that force players to break server rules
- laws that remove NLR, raid, building, or staff rules

### GOV-011 - Lockdowns need serious RP reason

Lockdowns are for major city threats, bank robberies, organized violence, public danger, or other real emergencies.

Do not use lockdowns for boredom, tax protests, punishing one player, or making the streets easier to farm.

### GOV-012 - Taxes and grants must be civic

The mayor may set taxes and issue treasury grants through the city tools, but those powers are audited and should serve city RP.

Do not set extreme taxes or grants purely to enrich friends, punish enemies, or drain treasury before leaving office.

### GOV-013 - Lottery must be fair

City lottery must use the official lottery system. Do not fake lottery adverts, manually collect buy-ins, or change terms after people enter.

### GOV-014 - Demotions need valid roleplay or rule reason

Use demotion votes for real job abuse, failure to perform role duties, or serious RP reasons.

Do not demote because you lost a fight, dislike a player, want their job slot, or disagree OOC.

### GOV-015 - Mayor and law command are visible targets

The mayor and law command can be opposed through valid roleplay, politics, votes, raids if allowed by current systems, and legal conflict.

They are not automatically KOS because they hold office.

## Key Numbers

- Default wanted duration: 300 seconds.
- ATM hack wanted duration: 30 seconds.
- Default warrant duration: 300 seconds.
- Law request expiry: 300 seconds.
- Custom laws: up to 10.
- Custom law length: up to 200 characters.

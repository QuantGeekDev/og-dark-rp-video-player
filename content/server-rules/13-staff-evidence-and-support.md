# Staff, Evidence, and Support

## In One Breath

Staff are here to keep the server playable, not to referee every bruised ego with a clipboard and a tiny bell.

## Rules

### STAFF-001 - Report serious or repeated issues

Use staff help for harassment, exploit abuse, repeated RDM, repeated random arrest, serious metagaming, major prop abuse, stuck/broken systems, and unresolved rule breaks.

Normal RP losses should usually stay in RP.

### STAFF-002 - Provide useful evidence

Good reports include:

- who was involved
- what happened
- where it happened
- when it happened
- what mechanic was involved
- video, screenshots, or logs when available

### STAFF-003 - Do not spam reports

Repeated reports without new information slow staff down and can become abuse.

If staff close a report, do not reopen the same issue endlessly unless you have new evidence.

### STAFF-004 - Do not weaponize reports

Do not bait players into rule breaks, clip only misleading fragments, lie to staff, coordinate false reports, or use staff sits as revenge.

Bad reports can be punished too.

### STAFF-005 - Stay still and clear during staff scenes

If staff pull you aside, answer questions, do not run, do not attack, do not destroy evidence, and do not invite friends to interrupt.

Being in a staff scene does not make you immune to consequences from lying or leaving.

### STAFF-006 - Exploit reports go private

Do not post exploit instructions in OOC, public chat, Discord public channels, signs, media, or streams.

Tell staff what happened, what you clicked, what you gained, and how urgent it is.

### STAFF-007 - Bug reports are not refund guarantees

Active development means bugs can happen. Staff may help when possible, but a bug report does not automatically guarantee refunds, item restoration, or scene reversal.

Clear evidence makes help more likely.

### STAFF-008 - Staff may use logs and audits

Admin logs, law logs, treasury logs, organization logs, and other audit trails may be used to resolve reports.

Changing your name or leaving the server does not erase those trails.

### STAFF-009 - Staff discretion has limits

Staff should follow this rulebook, server policy, available evidence, and common sense.

If a decision seems wrong, appeal it calmly through the proper channel instead of continuing the fight in OOC.

### STAFF-010 - Development and moderation are different

Some issues need code changes, not instant staff action. Staff may document a bug or forward it to development instead of solving it in the moment.

Do not harass staff because a feature is unfinished.

## Worth Reporting

- repeated RDM or targeting
- random arrests or police abuse
- exploit use
- hateful conduct
- major prop/base abuse
- scam involving official systems
- broken mechanic with clear reproduction details

## Usually Not Worth Reporting

- one accidental crossfire hit
- losing a fair raid
- being taxed by the mayor within server limits
- bad casino luck
- a printer exploding because you ignored heat
- getting arrested after a valid wanted

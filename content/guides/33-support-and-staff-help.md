# Support and Staff Help

## Quick Start

- Ask in OOC for basic gameplay questions.
- Use support/report surfaces when a staff issue needs attention.
- Include who, what, where, and when.
- Stay calm and keep roleplay separate from bug reports.
- If a UI or NPC is broken, say which one and what you clicked.

## Details

- Staff tools are for moderation, debugging, and server support.
- Admin activity and audit systems help staff see consequential actions.
- Support panels can collect structured reports when available.
- Screenshots, clear names, and exact mechanic names help resolve problems quickly.
- Many issues are easier to fix when reported before a scene restart.

## Gotchas

- Staff help is not a shortcut around job, money, or law rules.
- Do not spam reports for normal roleplay losses.
- Do not share exploit details in public chat.
- Some active development issues may need a developer pass, not an instant staff fix.

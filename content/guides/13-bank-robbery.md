# Bank Robbery

## Quick Start

- Thief or Pro Thief starts the robbery at a ready vault.
- Robbers must stay near the vault until the timer completes.
- Other eligible criminals can assist under the crew rules.
- Police get alerted and can stop the robbery.
- Surviving robbers split stolen gold and sell or stash it.

## Details

- Leaving the robbery area too long can remove you from the crew.
- Dying, disconnecting, or being arrested can remove you too.
- Gold has a short crew-only pickup grace period before other criminals can take it.
- Law enforcement can confiscate world gold.
- Criminal and black market roles can pocket, drop, persist, and sell stolen gold when allowed.

## Gotchas

- Starting a robbery marks you as a major target.
- Robbery rewards depend on live rules and balance.
- Do not block or exploit the vault area.
- Police stopping a robbery can have their own reward flow.

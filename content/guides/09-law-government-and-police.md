# Law, Government, and Police

## Quick Start

- Police roles use the Police tab for wanted players, warrants, custody, requests, and roster.
- Normal officers can request wanteds and warrants.
- Law command can approve, deny, or auto-issue after validation.
- Use law radio to coordinate with eligible government roles.
- Arrest wanted criminals with the proper police tools.

## Details

- Civil Protection, Police Chief, SWAT roles, Mayor, and some support roles have different authority.
- Wanted requests and warrant requests are queued when the actor lacks command authority.
- Gun license requests are durable and can be reviewed by chief or staff.
- Bulletins and BOLO information help coordinate active police.
- Police snapshots are only sent to eligible viewers.
- Destroying a drug lab, microwave, or money printer pays any player 40% of its value; law enforcement receives 70%.
- Bitcoin miners are completely legal. Do not destroy, confiscate, or arrest over them just because they are mining.

## Gotchas

- Police tools do not make every law role identical.
- SWAT Medic and bodyguard roles can have support access without full arrest authority.
- Requests can expire or be blocked by duplicate pending requests.
- Handcuffed or arrested players cannot use law radio.

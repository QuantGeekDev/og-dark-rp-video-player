# Boards and Hoverboards

## Quick Start

- Buy a board from the Misc shop when available.
- Press E to mount.
- Use movement controls to ride.
- Use jump or boost controls if the variant supports them.
- Pocket your own empty board to carry it.

## Details

- Boards are single-user transport, not full vehicles.
- Skateboards and longboards use arcade movement and dismount rules.
- Hoverboards can have special levitation, stamina, fuel, or audio behavior.
- Ownership blocks other players from riding your board.
- Pocketed boards can survive through supported persistence flows.

## Gotchas

- You cannot pocket a board while someone is riding it.
- Hard impacts, bad tilt, or wipeout rules can dismount you.
- Boards do not use the full vehicle lock and co-owner system.
- Riding does not make you immune to law or combat rules.

# Mayor and City Tools

## Quick Start

- Mayor is a vote-backed government job.
- The Mayor tab appears when you are mayor or staff.
- Set city tax carefully. High tax affects the whole server.
- Use grants to spend treasury on players.
- Start law votes or lockdowns only for real roleplay reasons.

## Details

- Treasury persists separately from the mayor seat.
- Mayor death, resignation, demotion, or disconnect resets tax to default.
- Laws can be proposed through a city-wide vote.
- Lottery creates a timed buy-in window and resolves automatically.
- Police command and mayor tools overlap in some law flows, but they are not the same panel.

## Gotchas

- Mayor powers are visible and audited.
- Law proposals still pass through normal law validation.
- A lottery continues even if the mayor leaves during the window.
- Staff can override for moderation and support.

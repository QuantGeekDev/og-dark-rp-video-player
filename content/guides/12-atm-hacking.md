# ATM Hacking

## Quick Start

- Switch to ATM Hacker.
- Equip the ATM hacking tool.
- Find a valid ATM and start the hack.
- Hold the hack until it completes.
- Grab the payout and move before police respond.

## Details

- ATM hacking is a criminal role loop.
- The tool and target are validated by the host.
- A successful hack creates a cash reward and can harm other online bank accounts.
- Hacking can apply wanted consequences.
- Failed, interrupted, or invalid hacks should not pay out.

## Gotchas

- Do not start a hack you cannot defend.
- Police are expected to respond.
- The same ATM or player may be cooldown-gated.
- Hacking is loud and obvious by design when the system exposes feedback.

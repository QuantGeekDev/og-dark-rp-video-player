# Weapons and Attachments

## Quick Start

- Gun Dealer and Black Market roles unlock weapon shop paths.
- Buy single weapons, shipments, ammo, or special tools when your job allows it.
- Use shipments to supply other players.
- Use the attachment menu on supported weapons.
- Pocket dropped supported weapons to save state when allowed.

## Details

- Weapons have server-side stats and ammo rules.
- Attachments can modify handling, optics, lasers, or other weapon behavior.
- Saved loadouts help reapply attachment setups.
- Black market tools can include criminal equipment that normal shops do not sell.
- Gun licenses affect legal status, not every server-side purchase rule.

## Gotchas

- Default and job weapons may be locked from dropping.
- Shipments are not directly equipable from pocket.
- Attachments cost money and can be job or item restricted.
- Owning a weapon does not make illegal use safe from police.

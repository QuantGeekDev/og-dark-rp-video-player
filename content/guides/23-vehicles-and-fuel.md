# Vehicles and Fuel

## Quick Start

- Use the Car Dealer NPC or vehicle shop flow to buy vehicles.
- Enter a valid seat with E.
- Drive with movement controls and use handbrake/turbo if the vehicle supports them.
- Use keys to lock or unlock your vehicle.
- Refuel with gas pumps, cans, or gas station systems.

## Details

- Vehicles use ownership rules similar to doors.
- Driver and passenger seats can have different behavior.
- Fuel drains with engine work and reaches zero instead of deleting the car.
- Gas Station Owner supports the fuel economy.
- Vehicle stats describe speed, acceleration, handling, braking, fuel, and seats.

## Gotchas

- Locked vehicles can still have raid or law interactions.
- Passengers are not the driver.
- Fuel cans hold limited liters.
- Bad parking can block streets and attract staff attention.

# Lootboxes and Radiants

## Quick Start

- Use the Lootbox Dealer UI when available.
- Cases and keys stack on your player state.
- Open cases for cash, fragments, inventory rewards, or permanent unlocks.
- Claim delayed rewards from the locker.
- Use radiant upgrades on supported machines.

## Details

- Cash rewards can be taxed when paid out.
- Duplicate permanent unlocks can convert into fragments.
- Locker rewards can include weapons, shipments, ammo, or upgrade parts.
- Radiants are rare machine modifiers for systems like printers and bitcoin miners.
- Reward catalogs are server-controlled.

## Gotchas

- Skills do not improve lootbox rarity or payout luck.
- Permanent unlocks are bound and not normal trade items.
- Pending money should only be taxed once.
- Explosive, police, build, or authority tools may be excluded by catalog policy.

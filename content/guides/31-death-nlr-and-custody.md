# Death, NLR, and Custody

## Quick Start

- Death can drop some wallet cash.
- Respawn and do not rush back into the same conflict.
- NLR can block returning to or interacting near your death area.
- Arrest moves you into custody rules.
- Bank money and pocket carefully to reduce losses.

## Details

- Death cash loss is calculated by server rules and spawned as a money stack.
- Large wallet balances can lose more than the basic death drop.
- NLR state can affect combat and interaction checks.
- Arrested players can miss salary, lose radio access, and wait out custody.
- Some skills or systems may reduce penalties within strict caps.

## Gotchas

- Dying is not a clean reset for revenge.
- Wallet cash is riskier than bank balance.
- Custody state can block tools, radio, and job behavior.
- Do not exploit respawn timing to avoid roleplay consequences.

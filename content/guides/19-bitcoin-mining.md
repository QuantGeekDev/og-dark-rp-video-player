# Bitcoin Mining

## Quick Start

- Buy a Bitcoin Miner and Generator from the Misc shop when eligible.
- Place the generator near the miner.
- Press E on the miner to open its console.
- Upgrade, repair, cool, and sell stored BTC when the price is good.
- Keep the setup protected.

## Details

- The current system uses one personal miner and one generator per player.
- Mining depends on generator power.
- The BTC spot price moves within server bounds.
- Miner upgrades improve production, storage, heat, and durability.
- Physical upgrade parts can flow through the pocket system.
- Radiant sockets can add rare machine effects.
- Bitcoin miners are completely legal. Mining BTC is not contraband.

## Gotchas

- Police jobs are blocked from buying miners and parts.
- That police purchase restriction does not make civilian bitcoin miners illegal.
- Overheated miners can catch fire or explode.
- Disconnect liquidation protects stored value when the feature path supports it.
- Selling during a weak BTC price can be worse than waiting.

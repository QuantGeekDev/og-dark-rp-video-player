# Mining and Refining

## Quick Start

- Switch to Miner.
- Use your pickaxe on mining veins.
- Pocket ore stacks when possible.
- Sell raw ore to the foreman or refine it into wafers.
- Use the IT buyer for premium high-tier ore and wafers.

## Details

- Mining has persistent progression and pickaxe upgrades.
- Different veins can favor different ore tiers.
- Ore and wafers merge into pocket stacks by type.
- Refining converts ore into higher-value wafers.
- Ore refiners and mining buyers have their own UI and status rules.

## Gotchas

- Pocket overflow can spawn drops instead of storing everything.
- Low-tier goods may be refused by premium buyers.
- Upgrades and skills affect the loop, but do not remove the need to mine.
- Watch your route when carrying valuable stacks.

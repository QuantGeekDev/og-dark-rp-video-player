# Shops, Vendors, and Selling

## Quick Start

- Open Tab, then Shop.
- Pick a category and buy items your job can use.
- If a shop looks empty, your job probably cannot buy that category.
- Talk to NPC buyers to sell ore, wafers, weed, or other supported goods.
- Watch the final notice for tax and net payout.

## Details

- Shop prices are validated by the host.
- Most purchases charge tax and credit the treasury.
- Many placed objects assign ownership when spawned.
- Some shops enforce per-player caps.
- Some goods can be pocketed and sold later.
- Waypoints can help you find NPCs and city services.

## Gotchas

- Buying an item does not mean you can use it forever after changing jobs.
- Failed spawns should refund without taxing.
- Police and government jobs can be blocked from criminal machines.
- Some sell paths pay less or refuse low-tier goods.

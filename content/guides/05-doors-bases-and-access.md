# Doors, Bases, and Access

## Quick Start

- Look at a door and press E to buy, rent, lock, or unlock when allowed.
- Use your keys tool for door and vehicle lock actions.
- Buy a base before placing valuable machines inside.
- Share access only with people you trust.
- Government and job doors may follow special access rules.

## Details

- Door ownership is server-authoritative.
- Owners, co-owners, organization access, and admin access are checked by the host.
- Police and raiders can interact with doors through tools, warrants, or breach rules.
- Some doors auto-lock for government use.
- Shared access helps friends use your base without giving away full ownership.

## Gotchas

- A locked door is not a perfect shield.
- Lockpicks, battering rams, warrants, and fading door devices all matter.
- If you change jobs, some job-specific access can change.
- Do not build in a way that blocks required map flow or staff access.

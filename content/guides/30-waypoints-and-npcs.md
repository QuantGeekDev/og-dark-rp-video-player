# Waypoints and NPCs

## Quick Start

- Open Tab, then Waypoints.
- Choose an available target.
- Follow the compass and distance HUD.
- Clear the waypoint when you are done.
- Use NPCs for selling, buying, jobs, media, and special services.

## Details

- Waypoints are generated from available scene targets.
- Some waypoints only appear for the right job, system state, or scene setup.
- Mining foreman, IT buyer, car dealer, weed buyer, and lootbox dealer are examples of guided targets.
- The compass updates distance and direction while active.
- Some NPCs use popups; others use direct E interactions.

## Gotchas

- A disabled waypoint will show why when possible.
- Target availability can change during play.
- Waypoints guide you to systems, but they do not reserve the NPC for you.
- If a target is missing, the scene placement may need staff or developer attention.

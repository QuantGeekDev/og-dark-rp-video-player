# Theft and Raiding

## Quick Start

- Thief and Pro Thief are the main raid roles.
- Use lockpicks on valid doors and fading doors.
- Use keypad crackers on keypad-protected bases.
- Use the pickpocket tool on players when the rules allow it.
- Expect police attention when crimes succeed or become obvious.

## Details

- Pro tools are faster or more capable than basic tools.
- Lockpicking and cracking are timed actions.
- Some doors or vehicles have special access, warrant, or ownership checks.
- Pickpocketing has target rules and anti-farm protections.
- Criminal tools work best with planning, not public spam.

## Gotchas

- Not every prop, door, or player can be targeted.
- Getting caught can reveal you or trigger roleplay consequences.
- Police can use tasers, cuffs, wanted status, and warrants.
- Staff may intervene if raiding becomes rule-breaking instead of roleplay.

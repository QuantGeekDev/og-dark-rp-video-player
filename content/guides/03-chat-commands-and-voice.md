# Chat, Commands, and Voice

## Quick Start

- Normal chat is local proximity chat.
- Use `/ooc message` for global out-of-character chat.
- Use `/pm player message` for private messages.
- Use `/advert message` for a public roleplay advertisement.
- Hold V for normal voice.
- Law radio roles can hold B for radio voice.

## Details

- `/y` yells farther than normal chat.
- `/w` whispers nearby.
- `/me` sends a local roleplay action.
- `/g` is job or group chat. For law-radio roles it routes to law radio.
- `/radio` or `/r` sends law radio text if your job is eligible.
- `/balance` shows wallet and bank balances.
- Job commands like `/citizen`, `/cp`, and `/mayor` are driven by job assets.

## Gotchas

- Unknown slash commands fail closed and do not echo into public chat.
- Arrested or handcuffed players cannot use law radio.
- Some commands are permission-gated by job or staff access.
- Built-in command names are reserved, so jobs cannot silently override them.

# Organizations

## Quick Start

- Organizations are persistent player groups.
- Join one to get a tag, group chat, shared identity, and possible upgrades.
- Leaders and officers manage invites, ranks, and treasury.
- Members can contribute money or activity.
- Use org tools only with people you trust.

## Details

- Organization actions are permission-based and audited.
- Ranks can control invite, kick, treasury, upgrades, and settings access.
- Treasury supports deposits, withdrawals, caps, and upgrade purchases.
- Tags can appear in chat, scoreboard, and name displays.
- Future or active shared storage features connect with pocket rules.

## Gotchas

- Leaving, kicking, and disbanding are consequential.
- Tags and names are validated to prevent impersonation.
- Treasury withdrawals should be limited and logged.
- Organization benefits must not replace job or law rules.
